Pinterest Search

Product Design, Motion, UI

Designing for end consumers, specifically those who use the search function on the Pinterest mobile app, I helped solve a few problems, including:
• Lift daily active searcher metric
• Find products users want but they don’t know what they are called
• Improve visual language consistency across search products
As a product & UI design intern on the search team, I explored through motion, video prototyping, interaction, and visual design on search suggestions on home feed, camera search, and in-pin visual search. Collaborating with 1 iOS eng and 1 PM, I help lift daily active searchers by 2% and shape the launch of Pinterest Lens.
