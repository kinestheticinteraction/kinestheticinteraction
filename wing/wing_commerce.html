Drone Network Interface Web App

Product design, UX, UI

Designing for internal users at Wing, I helped improve operational efficiency by solving a few pain points in its new business model, including:
• Drone network status can only be manually queried
• There is no visibility into the health of the overall drone network
• There is no visibility into service level at a region or partner site
• There is no visibility into what and how many nodes are at a partner site
As a lead product designer, I worked with 3 ENG, 1 PM, and 1 UXR to design this tool from scratch to provide visibility and transparency of the drone network throughout the company, from the business team to the tech org.
