Scalable UI for B2B2C Business Model

Wing provides APIs and UI widgets for external partners to integrate into their existing services. End consumers can then order goods from partners and Wing's autonomous drone fleet will pick up and deliver to consumers within minutes.

Example: Nike x Wing Delivery Onboarding

To expand Nike's delivery fleet and network into the sky, this easy to use 2-step UI widget seamlessly integrates into their app for reviewing Terms of Service and selecting a delivery spot. The best part is, this is done once and will be all set.

Prototype made in Framer Web with javascript, React, and typescript.
